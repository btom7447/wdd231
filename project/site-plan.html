Abkon Space

Workspace Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Abkon Space - A premium workspace hub for professionals, entrepreneurs, and creatives.

Optional domain availability: abkonspace.com

Site Purpose

The Abkon Space website will serve as the primary digital platform for:

  • Showcasing the workspace facilities and amenities
  • Providing information about membership plans and pricing
  • Displaying available services (private offices, coworking spaces, meeting rooms)
  • Promoting upcoming events and workshops
  • Facilitating online bookings and inquiries

Scenarios

These are typical questions that potential members might ask:

What types of workspace options are available, and how do they differ in price?

The website will provide detailed information about each workspace option (private offices, dedicated desks, hot desks, meeting rooms) with clear pricing structures and comparison features.

What amenities and services are included with membership?

The site will feature a comprehensive amenities section highlighting high-speed internet, printing services, coffee bars, networking events, and other member benefits.

How can I book a tour or schedule a day pass to try the space?

The website will include an online booking system for tours and day passes, with calendar integration for availability.

Typography

The typography has been selected to ensure readability while maintaining a modern, professional appearance.

Montserrat

Used for all headings (H1-H6) to create strong visual hierarchy and brand recognition.

Figtree

Used for body text, paragraphs, and navigation items for optimal readability at various sizes.
